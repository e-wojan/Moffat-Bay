Welcome to Moffat Bay Lodge

The Moffat Bay Lodge and Marina is a luxurious private retreat nestled on the southern shore of Joviedsa Island. The lodge overlooks the pristine beauty of Moffat Bay. The main lodge features spacious updated rooms, as well as an indoor pool, and lounge run by ICE certified mixologist John Wick. Rooms include kitchenettes, private bathrooms, electric fireplaces, air conditioning, hard wood floors throughout, vaulted ceilings with extravagant woodwork from local sculptors, private porch area, flat screen televisions and fiber speed internet connections. Laundry service is available upon request .
